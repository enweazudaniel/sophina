Admissions

Age Criteria

  • To get an admission for your child for Nursery, he/she must be 2 years old as on 1st April of the academic year. To get an admission for your child for KG, he/she must be 4 years old as on 1st April of the academic year.
  • Students seeking admission to classes KG-X, concept check will be conducted.
  • Admissions will be based on their aptitude test.


Documents required for registration

  1. Birth certificate copy of the child.
  2. Transfer certificate or result from the previous school.
  3. Two (2) Passport size color Photographs of the child.
